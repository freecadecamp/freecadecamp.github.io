The Science of Clean Cooking

Unlike traditional chulhas that produce harmful smoke, our advanced systems ensure complete combustion through optimized air flow and secondary ignition — resulting in a clean blue flame and near-zero particulate emissions.

1

Biomass fuel (pellets, chips, agri-waste) is loaded into the combustion chamber

2

Primary air initiates gasification, releasing volatile gases

3

Secondary air injection ensures complete combustion of gases — no smoke, only blue flame

4

Continuous feeding models maintain steady heat for 12+ hours without reloading

Our Journey

Founded to End Smoke & Energy Poverty

Founded in 2012, VARAM CLEAN COOKING emerged from a simple yet powerful vision: replace traditional smoky chulhas with efficient, smokeless biomass stoves that protect health and save money.

Over the last decade, we have expanded into continuous feeding systems for commercial kitchens, biogasifiers that convert organic waste into methane, and high-efficiency pellet stoves. Today, our solutions power thousands of households, hotels, canteens, and industries across India — all while reducing carbon emissions and promoting rural livelihoods through decentralized biomass utilization.
