Preparation

Pre-Installation Checklist

To ensure a smooth installation process, please prepare the following:

  • Adequate space for stove/hopper/digester placement
  • Proper exhaust/ chimney connection (if required)
  • Electrical outlet (for pellet stoves/auto-feed systems)
  • Biomass fuel stock (pellets, chips, agri-waste)
  • For biogasifier: organic waste supply & water source

Installation Guide

Installation by Product Type

Smokeless Stove

Simple plug-and-play. Requires stable surface and basic ventilation. Installation in 2-3 hours.

Continuous Feeding Stove

Hopper assembly, conveyor setup, electrical connection. Takes 1-2 days for commercial models.

Biogasifier

Digester installation, inlet/outlet piping, gas storage. Site excavation may be required.

Pellet Stove

Electrical connection, exhaust pipe, control panel setup. Installation in 4-5 hours.
