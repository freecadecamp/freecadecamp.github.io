Preparation

Pre-Installation Checklist

To ensure a smooth installation process, please prepare the following:

  • Adequate space (minimum 10x10 ft area)
  • Proper ventilation and exhaust setup
  • Electrical connection (230V / 415V as per model)
  • Water supply for cooling (if applicable)
  • Biomass feedstock availability (wood chips, pellets, etc.)
