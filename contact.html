Frequently Asked Questions

Which stove is best for home cooking?

Our Smokeless Biomass Stove is perfect for daily home use. It eliminates 98% smoke, uses twigs/pellets/chips, and saves 60% fuel compared to traditional chulhas.

What is a continuous feeding stove?

It's a commercial stove with a large hopper that automatically feeds biomass, providing 12+ hours of uninterrupted cooking — ideal for hotels, canteens, and marriage halls.

How does a biogasifier work?

A biogasifier converts kitchen waste, cow dung, or agricultural residue into methane gas through anaerobic digestion. The gas can be used for cooking, replacing LPG.

Are pellet stoves automatic?

Yes, our premium pellet stoves feature auto-ignition, digital temperature control, and programmable timers. They offer 95% efficiency with minimal ash.

What is the payback period?

Depending on usage and current fuel, payback is typically 6-18 months. Commercial users recover investment faster due to higher fuel savings.

Do you provide installation & support?
