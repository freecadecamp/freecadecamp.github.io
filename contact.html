Frequently Asked Questions

Which stove is best for home cooking?

Our Smokeless Biomass Stove is perfect for daily home use. It eliminates 98% smoke, uses twigs//chips, and saves fuel compared to traditional chulhas.

What is a stove?

It's a commercial stove with a large hopper that automatically feeds biomass, providing 12+ hours of uninterrupted cooking — ideal for hotels, canteens, and marriage halls.

How does a biogas work?

A biogas converts kitchen waste, cow dung, or agricultural residue into methane gas through anaerobic digestion. The gas can be used for cooking, replacing LPG.

Are automatic?

Yes, our feature auto-ignition, digital temperature control, and programmable timers. They offer 95% efficiency with minimal ash.

What is the payback period?

Depending on usage and current fuel, payback is typically 6-18 months. Commercial users recover investment faster due to higher fuel savings.

Our Mission

About VSS

VSS has been operating robustly since 2020, dedicated to carbon‑free, environment‑friendly, energy‑saving, and economically viable products. We manufacture high‑efficiency smokeless biomass stoves that empower households, hotels, and industries to cook clean, safe, and sustainably. As per NFHS-5 (2019-21): 41% of India still rely on biomass for cooking — emitting 340 million tonnes of CO₂ annually. Globally 2.4 billion people lack access to clean cooking solutions, causing significant damage to economy, public health and environment. Indoor air pollution from wood-based cooking causes about 3 million premature deaths annually worldwide, with 0.6 million in India (PTI report Jan 29, 2024).
